## 📝 App Details (500 characters max)
*Copy this plain text exactly. Do not add bolding or bullet points.*

Retner Smart Push enables merchants to send web push notifications to customers on desktop and mobile devices. This tool helps you re-engage visitors who have left your website by delivering messages directly to their screens. You can use the app to recover abandoned carts, announce flash sales, and send personalized updates. The system includes automated welcome messages and a dashboard for managing subscribers. This helps maintain customer retention without relying solely on email.
